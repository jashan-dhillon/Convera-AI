What Does Our Platform Do?

Conversational Intelligence

Talk to our system like you would to a colleague. It adapts in real time to your answers, asking right follow ups to capture what your agent need goal, constraints, workflow and tone.

Automated Knowledge Base Builder

No more manual formatting or writing documents. We convert yours conversation into a clean , structured knowledge base - tailored for direct use in low-code AI platform like flowise, langchain or relevance AI.

Agent - Ready Output

You bring the ideas - we handle rest. Export your knowledge base in multiple formats(JSON, Markdown,etc) or push it directly into your favourite agent builder.
